Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Personal Information

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide when joining Elite Health Club, including:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Identity number for membership verification
  • Emergency contact information
  • Payment information for membership fees
  • Health information relevant to your training

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process your membership and manage your account
  • Provide personalized training services
  • Communicate important club updates
  • Ensure your safety and emergency contact
  • Process payments and send receipts

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized staff.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
